## Orange App

Orange App is a finance app built during a makeathon that combines payments, budgeting, and investment tracking in one place. It’s designed to feel simple and fast, kind of like a streamlined all-in-one money app.

---

## Problem

People usually use different apps to:

* Send money
* Track spending
* Monitor investments

Switching between multiple apps makes it harder to stay organized and get a clear picture of your finances.

---

## Solution

Orange App brings all of these features into one platform so users can manage their money more easily without constantly switching apps.

---

## Features

### Payments

Send and receive money between users
**Notes:** Fast transfers, simple UI, and optional payment notes for tracking what each transaction was for

### Budgeting

Track income and expenses, organize spending into categories
**Notes:** Basic budgeting system with categories and visual breakdowns of spending

### Investment Tracking

View real-time stock prices and track portfolio performance
**Notes:** Live price updates and simple portfolio overview for quick insights

### Dashboard

View balance, transactions, budget, and investments in one place
**Notes:** Clean, centralized dashboard showing everything at a glance
